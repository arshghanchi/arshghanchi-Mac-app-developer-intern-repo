## 1. Professional vs. Unprofessional Behaviour in a Casual Work Environment

### **Professional behaviour includes:**
- Clear and respectful communication  
- Coming prepared to meetings with notes, questions, and progress updates  
- Following up on tasks and keeping teammates updated  
- Giving and receiving feedback with a constructive mindset  
- Asking thoughtful questions after doing basic research  
- Staying accountable and proactive  
- Respecting working hours, deadlines, and communication processes  
- Being dependable and consistent  

### **Unprofessional behaviour includes:**
- Showing up to meetings unprepared  
- Ignoring feedback or repeating the same errors  
- Delayed or missing updates (‚Äúghosting the project‚Äù)  
- Asking questions that could be Googled in 10 seconds  
- Sending vague or confusing calendar invites  
- Not taking notes and repeatedly asking the same thing  
- Using an inappropriate tone in messages  
- Responding emotionally or defensively instead of seeking clarity

## 2. What Respectful Communication Looks Like in Remote Teams

- Avoiding sarcasm or ambiguous wording  
- Giving teammates time to respond‚Äîrespecting async culture

## 3. Giving and Receiving Feedback Professionally

### **Giving feedback:**
- Focus on behaviours and actions, not personality  
- Be specific: *what* needs improvement and *why*  
- Assume good intent everyone wants to do well  
- Provide examples and suggestions  

### **Receiving feedback:**
- Listen fully before responding  
- Ask clarifying questions if needed  
- Avoid reacting defensively  
- Apply the feedback and show change  
- Thank the person for helping you improve
